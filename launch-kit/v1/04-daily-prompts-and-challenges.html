Daily Prompts and Challenges

Use these prompts between live sessions. They create a steady rhythm of reflection, practice, verification, and public proof.

Weekly challenges

WeekChallengeHomework
Week 1: AI, Agency, and the Work AheadAsk an AI tool to explain a task you already know. Mark what is useful, shallow, wrong, and missing.Draft a one-page AI use statement: what I will use AI for, what I will not use it for, and how I will disclose it.
Week 2: Prompting as Work DesignRewrite three weak prompts into useful briefs using context, task, constraints, example, and review criteria.Create four reusable prompts: draft, critique, summarize, and plan.
Week 3: Verification and Source DisciplineHave AI summarize a public article, then compare the output to the source and correct errors.Build a five-step verification checklist for your role track.
Week 4: Workflow MappingMap one recurring workflow with inputs, steps, decisions, handoffs, risks, and outputs.Interview one person who knows the workflow and add what they need to trust the result.
Week 5: Role Lab: Communication and OperationsConvert messy notes into an action summary with owners, dates, open questions, and risks.Create one reusable communication template and test it with a peer.
Week 6: Role Lab: Data, Research, and DecisionsCompare three options for a real decision and document what still needs human review.Create a one-page decision memo using at least three trusted sources.
Week 7: Accessibility and Inclusion by DefaultRevise one document for plain language, screen reader structure, and multiple reading levels.Run an inclusion review with one person who is not like you.
Week 8: Automation with GuardrailsDesign a low-risk automation and explain where humans remain in the loop.Write do-not-automate rules for your portfolio workflow.
Week 9: Portfolio Sprint OneBuild the smallest useful AI-assisted version of the workflow.Test the prototype with one stakeholder and capture notes.
Week 10: Portfolio Sprint TwoRun two tests: one quality test and one usefulness test.Revise the prototype and update the implementation guide.
Week 11: Story, Demo, and Role PathwayDeliver a five-minute demo that includes what worked, what failed, and what should stay human.Record a practice demo and request feedback from a peer.
Week 12: Demo Day and Next Cohort HandoffPublish or privately share the portfolio with a clear disclosure and ask.Update the local playbook with one thing the next cohort should inherit.
