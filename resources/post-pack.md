# The AI Kit Post Pack

## Flagship Launch Post

AI is changing work faster than most institutions can redesign programs.

So we built the program now.

The AI Kit is a 12-week, community-run curriculum that helps adult learners, community college students, nonprofit staff, job seekers, and frontline teams build practical AI capability through real work.

The goal is not to make everyone a machine learning engineer.

The goal is to help people become more capable in the roles communities already need:

- Operations assistants who can improve messy workflows
- Student services navigators who can find accurate answers faster
- Marketing and content operators who can draft, test, and revise responsibly
- Data and story analysts who can turn information into useful decisions
- Nonprofit capacity builders who can save staff time without removing human care

Every learner leaves with:

- A personal AI use statement
- A prompt and verification library
- A real workflow improvement project
- A portfolio demo
- A recommended role or apprenticeship pathway

Every local host gets:

- A 12-week curriculum
- Facilitator guidance
- Employer project templates
- AI use norms
- Measurement tools
- Launch posts and partner emails

This is not a funding announcement. It is an implementation invitation.

Community colleges, libraries, workforce boards, nonprofits, employers: here is your program. Start with 20 to 30 learners, three real project partners, and a demo day. Publish what happens. Improve it. Let the next community copy it.

AI's gains will not become broadly shared by accident. Capability has to be distributed on purpose.

## Employer Post

Most employers do not need a vague AI upskilling initiative.

They need people who can improve real workflows with judgment:

- Drafting with review
- Research with source checks
- Intake with escalation
- Reporting with caveats
- Documentation that another human can trust

The AI Kit gives employers a simple way to participate.

Bring one recurring workflow. Work with a learner. Review the demo. Use the role brief to sponsor an apprenticeship or entry-level AI-enabled role.

This is how AI skills become jobs instead of buzzwords.
